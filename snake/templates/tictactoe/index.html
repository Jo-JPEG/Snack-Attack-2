Welcome to Tic Tac Toe! It's a game where you have to get three of your marks in a row to win. You can play against another player or the computer. Good luck!

How to play:
1. Click on a square to place your mark
2. Get three of your marks in a row to win
3. Block your opponent from getting three in a row
